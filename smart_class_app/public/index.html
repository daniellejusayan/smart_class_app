Core Features

Everything you need for
reliable attendance

📍

GPS Verification

Automatically captures the student's location at check-in and checkout to confirm physical presence in class.

📷

QR Code Scanning

Students scan a unique class QR code shown by the instructor — preventing remote or proxy attendance entirely.

🧠

Learning Reflections

Pre-class and post-class reflection forms encourage active engagement and give instructors insight into student understanding.

😄

Mood Tracking

A 5-point mood scale captures how students feel before each class, helping identify patterns over time.

💾

Offline Storage

All data is stored locally on the device using SQLite. Records persist even if the app is closed or the network drops.

🎓

Instructor Portal

A standalone web page lets instructors generate and display class QR codes with optional expiry timers — no app required.

Student Flow

Four simple steps

1

Open the App

Launch SmartClass and tap Check In when your class begins.

2

Verify GPS Location

The app captures your GPS coordinates to confirm you're physically on campus or in the classroom.

3

Scan the Class QR Code

Point your camera at the instructor's QR code to link your attendance to the specific class session.

4

Fill in Your Reflection

Answer a short reflection about the previous class and what you expect to learn today. Select your mood. Done!

5

Finish Class

At the end, tap Finish Class, scan the exit QR, and submit your post-class reflection with what you learned.
